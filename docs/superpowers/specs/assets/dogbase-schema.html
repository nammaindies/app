dogbase · data model

Schema v2 — every belief traces to an event

Target schema for the full v1 pipeline. A dog is recognized once by a model, then by a feeder, then by a vet — and each recognition is a row you can point to. Cards outlined in teal are what Slice 1 (collect & store) actually builds; the rest exist in the migration but stay empty until later slices.

The load-bearing invariant

sightings.individual_id and match_status are caches of the latest accepted decision — recomputable from match_proposals + confirmations. Every link, including automatic high-confidence ones, writes a proposal. That is what makes merges, splits, threshold recalibration, and “why does the system think this is Kaju?” all answerable — and what makes a feeder’s recognition both the product and the training label.
